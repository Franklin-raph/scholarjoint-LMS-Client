Features

Our Learning Management System provides you with the following features so your path in becoming a developer would be easier

Mobile Accessibility

Scholarsjoint Learning Management System be accessible on mobile devices so that students can access their courses anytime, anywhere.

Comprehensive Course Library

Scholarsjoint Learning Management System has has comprehensive library of courses that cover a wide range of topics and programming languages.

Interactive Learning Environment

Scholarsjoint Learning Management System gives you an interactive learning environment with features such as quizzes, coding challenges, and real-time feedback.

Collaborative Learning Tools

Scholarsjoint Learning Management System provides you with collaborative tools such as forums, chat rooms, and project collaboration tools to facilitate peer-to-peer learning.

Steps

To get started with our Learning Management System, all you need is to follow the steps listed below

STEP 1

Sign Up

Click on the sign up button above in the home section to get your self registered

STEP 2

Verify Email

Check your email and click on the link being sent to verify your account

STEP 3

Profile Update

Log in to your account and go to your settings to update your profile with the necessary details

STEP 4

Start Learning

After your profile has been updated, you can now return to your dashboard and select your track.
